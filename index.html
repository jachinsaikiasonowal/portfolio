The Problem
A B2B SaaS company spending $180K/year on a 3-person SDR team generating inconsistent pipeline. Manual lead qualification meant hours of research per prospect, outreach was generic, follow-up was sporadic. CAC was rising, conversion rate was declining, and the team was burning out on low-leverage work. The ask: replace the broken system, not add more headcount.
The Architecture
Designed a three-agent pipeline with closed feedback loops: a Researcher Agent (enriches every lead with company signals, tech stack, hiring patterns, recent news), a Scorer Agent (ranks leads by ICP fit and buy-signal strength), and a Writer Agent (generates context-aware, fully personalised outreach sequences). Every reply, click, and booking feeds back into the scorer — the system learns which signals convert and reweights automatically.
The Outcome
CAC dropped 61% within 90 days. Sales cycle compressed from 14 days to 5. The pipeline running 24/7 processes 400+ leads/week — what the SDR team handled monthly. Three human roles were redeployed to closing and expansion. The system doesn't plateau: every week of data makes the scoring model sharper and the outreach more precise.
System Architecture
01
Signal Mapping
Mapped 14 buy-signal categories: job postings, funding events, tech stack changes, leadership transitions. Each weighted by historical close data.
02
Researcher Agent
n8n workflow: pulls from Apollo, LinkedIn, Clearbit, and company news APIs. Enriches each lead record with 22 structured fields in under 90 seconds.
03
Scorer Agent
GPT-4o prompt chain evaluates ICP match and signal heat. Outputs a 0–100 score with a 3-line rationale. Only 70+ scores enter the outreach queue.
04
Writer Agent
Context-aware outreach generator. References the specific signal that triggered the outreach. 5-touch sequence: email → LinkedIn → email → call brief → final email.
05
Feedback Loop
All reply sentiment, open rates, and bookings flow back into HubSpot and retrain the scorer weekly. System gets measurably sharper every 7 days.

The newsletter existed but wasn't doing the job: moving subscribers toward activation, expansion, or advocacy.
The Architecture
Built a closed-loop content intelligence system with three layers: a Curator Agent (monitors 40+ sources, clusters topics by relevance signal), a Personalisation Engine (segments audience into 6 behavioural profiles based on click history and product usage), and a Synthesis Agent (writes each issue variant in brand voice, adapting angle and depth per segment). Click and reply data feeds back weekly — the system learns what content each segment actually wants.
The Outcome
Open rate from 24% → 52% in 11 weeks. Editorial time per issue: 6 hours → 45 minutes. Six personalised content variants per send, each performing 2–3× better than the previous single-version blast. $6,200 in direct newsletter-attributed revenue within 90 days from a targeted upsell sequence built into the highest-intent segment. The system gets better every week without additional effort.
